Zeitgeist Global Connect

A global website for the Zeitgeist Movement needs to: Awaken the conscience of potential new members with a clear and concise presentation of our urgent concern for the present world and of our vision for the future. Present the evidence, in summary and in detail, using primary sources and up-to-date supporting material of all types in clear and comprehensible formats, easily accessible by users of all types. Allow new members to orient themselves easily within the ZM in regards to chapters, projects and teams, and to provide clear paths to increased awareness, involvement, activism and collaboration. Enable active members to communicate globally. Provide an environment for members to focus appropriately, effectively and productively on the Movement's goals and strategies. Stimulate projects and collaborations among members, chapters and teams globally. Permit global coordination of our activities. Be a credible presence on the internet and help build links with other credible, compatible and parallel organizations and movements. Unify the ZM and embody the unity of the global human family and our mutual interconnection, regardless of national or other boundaries.
